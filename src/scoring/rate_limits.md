# Understand the Free Tier Limits (Gemini 2.5 Flash)

As of early 2026, the free tier for Gemini 2.5 Flash provides a generous testing environment, but it has specific "speed bumps" to prevent commercial abuse:
Rate Limits: 15 Requests Per Minute (RPM) and 250,000 Tokens Per Minute (TPM).
Daily Quota: Approximately 250 to 500 Requests Per Day (RPD), depending on your region.[5]
Context Window: You still get access to the massive 1-million-token context window.[5][10]
